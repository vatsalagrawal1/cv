Vatsal Agrawal

Founder Director of Vatsal School Of Music

Founder Director of MusiQon

Director of the most premier western music institute in Chhattisgarh, a multi-instrumentalist, Vatsal Agrawal, holds two Grade 8 Qualifications in Rock & Pop Guitar and Rock & Pop Vocals from Trinity College London. Operating a complete Music School with a passionate team of music teachers in Bhilai, he is known for his enthusiastic and innovative approach towards music education. To elevate the quality of Western music education in central India, he introduced internationally recognized graded qualifications from Trinity College London in Chhattisgarh. Vatsal is the representative of Rockschool London in Chhattisgarh. Vatsal is also a full member of Registry Of Guitar Tutors London.
